## Sorular
1. CentOS'ta varsayilan firewall servisi hangisidir?
   - A) ufw
   - B) firewalld
   - C) nftables
   - D) pf
   <details>
   <summary>Cevap</summary>
   firewalld
   </details>

2. SELinux modlari hangileridir?
   - A) Enforcing/Permissive/Disabled
   - B) Open/Closed
   - C) Kernel/User
   - D) A/B
   <details>
   <summary>Cevap</summary>
   Enforcing/Permissive/Disabled
   </details>

3. Paket yoneticisi hangisidir?
   - A) apt
   - B) dnf/yum
   - C) pacman
   - D) zypper
   <details>
   <summary>Cevap</summary>
   dnf/yum
   </details>
